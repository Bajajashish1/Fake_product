## 📸 **Photography Guide for Best Results**

### 🎯 **Essential Tips:**
1. **Lighting:** Use natural light or bright, even lighting
2. **Positioning:** Show the entire shoe including sole and brand labels
3. **Background:** Use plain, contrasting background (white/black works best)
4. **Focus:** Ensure the shoe is sharp and in focus
5. **Angle:** Take photos from multiple angles (side, front, sole)
6. **Distance:** Get close enough to show details but include the whole shoe

### 📊 **Quality Metrics You'll See:**
- **Sharpness Score:** How clear and focused your image is
- **Brightness Level:** Whether lighting is adequate  
- **Contrast Rating:** How well the shoe stands out from background
- **Color Diversity:** Richness of color information in the image
